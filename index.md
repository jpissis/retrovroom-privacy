# Privacy Policy — Retro Vroom

**Last updated:** March 17, 2026

## Overview

Retro Vroom ("the App") is a mobile game developed by Jérôme Pissis ("we", "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to transparency about data practices.

## Data We Collect

### Data we do NOT collect
- No personal information (name, email, phone number)
- No account or login required
- No location data
- No contacts, photos, or microphone access
- No user-generated content

### Data collected automatically
The App uses the following third-party services that may collect data:

**Google AdMob (Advertising)**
- Advertising identifier (IDFA) if you consent via the App Tracking Transparency prompt
- Device information and ad interaction data
- Purpose: to display relevant rewarded video ads
- Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

**Apple Game Center (Leaderboards)**
- Game scores submitted to leaderboards
- Managed entirely by Apple
- Apple's privacy policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

**Apple StoreKit (In-App Purchases)**
- Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple
- We do not have access to your payment information

### Local data storage
Game progress (scores, unlocked cars, coins, settings) is stored locally on your device using UserDefaults. This data is not transmitted to any server.

## Advertising

The App displays voluntary rewarded video ads only. No ads are ever forced. You choose to watch an ad in exchange for in-game rewards. VIP subscribers see no ads.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App contains no user-to-user communication, no social features, and no data collection beyond what is described above.

## Your Choices

- **Ad tracking:** You can opt out of personalized ads via iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking
- **Reset advertising ID:** iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising
- **Delete local data:** Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
